About

Hi there, my name is Sarah and after studying biology, I decided to go into science communication. During my freelance work as science journalist and photographer, I realised that if I wanted to truly push boundries and create innovative products and projects, I would have to become involved with the world of code. I already had a basic knowledge of HTML and CSS thanks to playing around with Wordpress and Adobe Muse but I wanted to take it a step further and be able to build more than a simple website.

Therefore, in summer 2019 I applied for the Ironhack Kleiderkreisel scholarship and was lucky enough to be granted one. And, come August, I was learning to master JavaScript and with the Ironhack coding bootcamp in Berlin.

Now that I have layed a first foundation in dealing with databases using Node.js, creating applications with ReactJS and have built my first own projects, I am looking to use and further my coding-skills as a web-developer.
